Texts:

  • Massey, "A Basic Course in Algebraic Topology", Springer 1991. (Required)
  • Singer and Thorpe, "Lecture Notes on Elementary Topology and Geometry", Springer 1967. (Recommended)

Syllabus:

  • The main part of the course will be foundational material on the fundamental group, covering spaces, and singular homology theory. (Roughly Chapters 1-12 of Massey.)
  • The first two weeks of the course will be a quick review/outline of the basics of point set topology. (Roughly the first 2 chapters of Singer and Thorpe.)
